Ham and Cheese Sandwich Recipe


Ham and Cheese sandwiches are a delicious food that taste pretty good. With this recipe your next ham and cheese sandwich is guaranteed to taste great! If you don't use this recipe, however, your sandwich will be nasty and anyone you make it for will hate you forever. So follow this quick n' easy recipe!

    Ingredients
  • Mustard
  • Ham
  • Swiss or Gruyere Cheese
  • Bread (No nasty whole grain)
    Steps
  1. Cut two slices of bread and place them down.
  2. Take slices of cheese and ham in a ratio of around 2.5 Ham to 1 Cheese and stack them on one slice of the bread.
  3. Spread mustard on the other slice of bread, as much as you want.
  4. Put the mustard bread on top of the meat-cheese bread and put it on a plate.
  5. Consume.
